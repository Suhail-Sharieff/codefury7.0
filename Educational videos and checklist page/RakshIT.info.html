NATURAL DISASTERS

Natural disasters include all types of severe weather, which have the potential to pose a significant threat to human health and safety, property, critical infrastructure, and homeland security.

This page provides some of the educational videos that will help you during the time of natural disaster.

1. Earthquake:

An earthquake is the sudden release of strain energy in the Earth's crust, resulting in waves of shaking that radiate outwards from the earthquake source.
